**Effective date:** 2026-06-24
**Developer:** Harikanth Lingutla
**Contact:** harikanth.ai@gmail.com

> This is a plain-language policy for the Kekasatori macOS app. It is a starting
> draft for your own review — have a professional confirm it before publishing.

## Summary
Kekasatori is a local-first macOS app. **We (the developer) do not operate a
server, do not have user accounts, and do not collect, transmit, or sell your
personal data.** Your content stays on your Mac except when *you* choose to send
it to a third-party service you configured (see "Third-party services").

## What stays on your device
All of the following are stored only locally on your Mac:
- Imported and downloaded media, papers, and articles.
- Generated study material (transcripts, summaries, notes, flashcards, mind
  maps, canvas boards, notebook entries) in a local database.
- App settings and recent links.
- **API keys** you enter for AI providers — stored in the macOS **Keychain**,
  never in plain files, never logged, and never sent anywhere except directly to
  the provider you configured.

## Third-party services (only when you use them)
Kekasatori contacts external services **only as a result of actions you take**:
- **AI providers (BYOK):** If you configure a "bring-your-own-key" provider
  (e.g. OpenRouter and the model you select), the text you ask about — including
  excerpts of the document or transcript — is sent to that provider using your
  key. Their privacy policy and terms govern that data. If you instead use Apple
  Intelligence (on-device), that processing happens locally on your Mac.
- **Content sources:** When you import or download from a URL (e.g. arXiv,
  PubMed, web articles, or media sites), the app connects to that source to
  retrieve the content you requested.
- **Software updates:** The app checks for its own updates and may update its
  bundled media tool. These requests retrieve files only; they do not send your
  personal data.

We receive **no copy** of any of this data.

## Analytics & tracking
None. Kekasatori contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking
SDKs.

## Children
Kekasatori is not directed to children under 13.

## Your control
Because everything is local, you can delete your data at any time by removing
items in the app or deleting the app's Application Support folder. Removing the
app removes its local data.

## Changes
We may update this policy; the effective date above will change accordingly.
